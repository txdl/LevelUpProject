Derek

I am a 23 year old male, who dropped out of high school. I'm a gamer with an interest in programming. I had no idea what I wanted to focus on until I found Level Up! They helped me focus on what I wanted to do. I now know I need to work on javascript and html is fine. I enjoy general design more than coding, something I never thought I would discover here. I know I can achieve what I want, my journey does not stop here it has only begun. They helped me decide which path I want to take and shown me what skills I need to improve. Now I can focus on the future and plan carefully with the knowledge and skills I've gained from here on out.
